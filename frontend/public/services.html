  • Key Features of ARMS:

    1. User Management:

    Securely manage user roles, profiles, and permissions with advanced authentication systems, including two-factor authentication.

    2. Project Management:

    Track progress, collaborate with team members, and archive completed projects effortlessly with a dedicated dashboard.

    3. Protocol Management:

    Create and refine review protocols using rich-text editors, templates, and seamless integration with platforms like PROSPERO.

    4. Search and Query Management:

    Design and translate search strategies across databases like PubMed and Scopus with automated query validation and visualizations.

    5. Study Screening and Selection:

    Dual-reviewer workflows ensure reliable screening of titles, abstracts, and full texts, with conflict resolution mechanisms and PRISMA-compliant outputs.

    6. Data Extraction:

    Customize extraction forms for quantitative and qualitative data, with support for independent data extraction and conflict resolution.

    7. Quality Assessment:

    Evaluate the methodological quality and risk of bias using customizable tools, with summary reports for comprehensive insights.

    8. Data Synthesis and Analysis:

    Conduct advanced meta-analyses, qualitative syntheses, and statistical modeling, complete with heterogeneity and sensitivity analyses.

    9. Reporting and Export:

    Generate PRISMA checklists and manuscripts in multiple formats, streamlining the publication process.

    10. Collaboration and Communication:

    Facilitate teamwork with real-time collaboration, task assignments, and commenting features.

    11. Communication and Version Control:

    Stay informed with task reminders, project updates, and version history tracking.

    12. System Security and Access Control:

    Protect sensitive data with encryption, role-based access control, and regular backups, ensuring compliance with privacy laws like GDPR.

    13. Auditing and History Tracking:
