About Us

Are you a researcher aiming to streamline your systematic reviews and meta-analyses? Look no further than ARMS—the Augmented Review Management System—a cutting-edge software solution designed to simplify, automate, and enhance every aspect of review studies. From traditional literature reviews to complex systemic review and meta-analyses, ARMS is here to revolutionize your research journey.

Discover ARMS: Transforming Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses for Researchers

ARMS designed to automate the management and execution of review studies and support researchers throughout the studies . ARMS will handle management of traditional literature reviews, scoping reviews, and various types of systematic reviews including both qualitative and quantitative review, mixed methods reviews, and meta-analysis. It will support the entire review process from project creation, planning, study selection, data extraction, to synthesis, analysis, and reporting.
